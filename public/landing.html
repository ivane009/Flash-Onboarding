Lightning Network

The Lightning Network is a second-layer solution that enables instant, low-cost Bitcoin transactions by creating payment channels between users. Transactions that would take minutes or hours on the main Bitcoin network are settled in seconds.

KYC Verification

Know Your Customer (KYC) is our identity verification process. We collect and verify your personal information to prevent fraud, money laundering, and ensure regulatory compliance. Your data is encrypted and protected with bank-level security.

Global Coverage

Flash operates in over 15 countries across Latin America and West Africa, with support for multiple local currencies including XOF, USD, EUR, and more. Our local payment partnerships ensure the best rates and fastest deposits.

Mobile Apps

Download our native iOS or Android app to manage your Bitcoin on the go. Receive instant notifications, track your transactions, and securely access your wallet with biometric authentication.
